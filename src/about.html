About Us

At TripGO, we believe that travel should be simple, affordable, and accessible to everyone. That's why we built a platform dedicated to making bus travel smarter and smoother for passengers across West Bengal and neighboring regions. Whether you're heading home for the weekend, traveling for work, or setting out on a new adventure, we're here to ensure that booking your bus is quick, reliable, and stress-free every single time.

Partnering With Government Transport

We’re proud to now support official government bus bookings — opening up more affordable, reliable travel for everyone. You can book state-run buses directly through our platform, with full transparency and confidence in the service. This is a huge step toward our mission of connecting cities, towns, and communities with safe and dependable public transport.

What Makes Us Different ?

Local & Reliable

Built by locals who understand your routes, needs, and challenges. We work with trusted operators to give you a reliable and personalized travel experience.

Instant Booking

No more long queues or last-minute panic. Our platform offers real-time seat availability, fast payments, and instant e-tickets — all in just a few taps.

Mobile Friendly

Our platform works great on all devices. Whether you're booking on the go or planning at home, enjoy a clean and responsive experience.

Our Coverage

We currently operate across the length and breadth of West Bengal through our reliable and affordable bus booking services. As we continue to grow, we’re actively working on expanding our network beyond West Bengal to serve more regions across Eastern India.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between traditional transport and modern technology, bringing digital convenience to millions of everyday travelers across India — starting right here in Bengal. We’re constantly evolving, adding new features, routes, and improvements to serve you better.

Thank You For Choosing Us

Every booking you make supports a growing movement toward smarter, more inclusive travel. We’re honored to be part of your journey and excited to keep moving forward with you.
