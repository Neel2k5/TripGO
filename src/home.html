Burdwan

Burdwan is known for its majestic palaces, ancient temples, and lush greenery. Explore the Rajbari Palace, the iconic 108 Shiva Temples, and experience the serene beauty of Bengal's heritage heartland.

Ajodhya

A hidden gem nestled in the Purulia district, Ajodhya Hills offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and tribal culture. With scenic waterfalls, rolling green hills, and adventure spots like trekking and rock climbing, it’s a serene escape for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike.

Malda

Known for the ancient cities of Gaur and Pandua, it showcases stunning Islamic architecture, moss-covered ruins, and centuries-old mosques. Add to that the region’s famous mangoes and silk, and you’ve got a unique blend of culture, history, and flavor
